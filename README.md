# india-ca-compliance-due-dates
Open-source compliance due dates repository for Chartered Accountants in India covering Income Tax, GST, TDS, PF, ESI, ROC, Professional Tax, FSSAI and other statutory filings.
# India CA Compliance Due Dates

This repository provides statutory compliance due dates applicable in India for:

- Income Tax
- TDS & TCS
- GST
- PF & ESI
- ROC Filings
- LLP Filings
- OPC Compliance
- Professional Tax (State-wise)
- Shops & Establishments
- FSSAI

The goal is to create the **largest open-source compliance calendar for Chartered Accountants in India**.
